Claims Express

Onsite Health Claims Approval



Objective

An insurance company has a mandate by government to provide health insurance to poor families and facilitate the claims processing from 72 hours to less than 4 hours. There should be no paper exchange. The insurance company had the processing agent with a laptop and connectivity in the hospital premises covered under the insurance plan. The objectives of this system are:

1. Doing away with the conventional paper based transactions for faster turn around time.

2. The claims processing time to be reduced from 72 hours to less than 4 hours.

3. The overall process becomes green with no paper flowing in, no faxes, no postal communication.

4. The poor and needy with health smart cards do not struggle at the hour of need.
